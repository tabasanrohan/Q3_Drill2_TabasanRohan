Emergency Go Bag Essentials

1.

FOOD & WATER

Pack non perishable canned goods for long shelf life, as well as a considerable supply of water.

2.

FIRST AID KIT

To lessen the damage of injuries, make sure to bring personal medication with you such as a GoBag.

3.

DOCUMENTS & CASH

Bring your important documents such as your identification cards and certificates, make sure to store them safely.

4.

HYGIENE & CLOTHES

Ensure that you have extra clothes and bring other personal hygenic products with you.
